What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) when you visit our website. They enable us to optimize site functionality, analyze user behavior, and deliver a personalized experience tailored to your preferences.

Types of Cookies We Use

We utilize various cookies to ensure a seamless and secure experience, including:

  • Essential Cookies: Critical for core website functionality, such as navigation and secure access.
  • Analytical Cookies: Allow us to analyze site usage to improve performance and content.
  • Functional Cookies: Enhance usability by remembering your preferences, such as language settings.
  • Marketing Cookies: Enable targeted advertising and campaign tracking (where applicable).

Managing Your Cookies

You have full control over cookies through your browser settings or our consent tool. Disabling essential cookies may impact website functionality. To manage your cookies:

  • Browser Settings: Adjust preferences in browsers like Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge.
  • Opt-Out Tools: Visit the Network Advertising Initiative or Digital Advertising Alliance to opt out of marketing cookies.
  • Our Consent Tool: Customize your preferences via our cookie consent popup upon visiting our site.
