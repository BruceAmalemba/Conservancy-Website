Our Timeless Heritage

Rooted in the heart of Luo tradition, Ambururu intertwines a legacy of culture, nature, and forward-thinking vision.

‘Kwe gi kow, yamo e piny’

Honor the land, and it will embrace you.

Sacred Luo Sanctuary

The waterfalls have long been a spiritual haven for the Luo people, serving as a site for rituals and community gatherings.

Nature’s Masterpiece

Over centuries, the Yala River has sculpted Ambururu’s majestic cascades, creating a natural wonder in Siaya’s landscape.

A Vision Born

Justice Anthony Ombwayo, inspired by his heritage and love for nature, envisioned an eco-resort that honors Siaya’s beauty and culture.

Global Retreat

Ambururu opens its doors as a world-class eco-luxury destination, welcoming guests to experience the harmony of tradition and innovation.

Our Visionary Founder

Justice Anthony Ombwayo

A distinguished Kenyan conservationist and visionary leader, Justice Ombwayo founded Ambururu to celebrate Luo heritage and empower Siaya’s communities through sustainable tourism.

With a career dedicated to environmental stewardship, Justice Ombwayo has led initiatives that employed over 75 locals, supported reforestation efforts with 1500+ trees planted, and promoted Luo craftsmanship, earning him recognition as a champion of eco-tourism in Kenya.
