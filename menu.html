At Ambururu Waterfalls Conservancy, dining transcends mere sustenance—it is a celebration of Kenya’s vibrant ecosystems and the timeless traditions of the Luo people. Each dish is a masterpiece, crafted with ingredients sourced from Siaya’s fertile lands and Lake Victoria’s pristine waters, honoring our commitment to conservation and heritage. We invite you to embark on this culinary odyssey, where every bite weaves a story of luxury, legacy, and environmental stewardship.

Our Heritage & Conservation Mission

Ambururu Waterfalls Conservancy is a sanctuary where Luo traditions intertwine with a profound commitment to preserving Siaya’s natural beauty. Our cuisine celebrates the Luo people’s deep connection to Lake Victoria and the Yala Valley’s fertile lands. We source 90% of our ingredients from local farmers and fishermen, practicing zero-waste cooking and using biodegradable packaging. Every dish reflects our dedication to sustainability, supporting conservation efforts to protect Kenya’s ecosystems for future generations.
