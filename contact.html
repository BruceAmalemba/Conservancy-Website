Frequently Asked Questions

How can I make a reservation?

You can book via our website, email reservations@ambururuwaterfalls.com, or call +254 720 215 511.

What is the best way to reach the resort?

The resort is accessible via the Kisumu-Siaya road. We offer shuttle services from Kisumu Airport.

Do you offer event planning services?

Yes, we provide tailored event planning for weddings and festivals.

Are pets allowed?

No, pets are not permitted to protect the ecosystem.
