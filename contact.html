Frequently Asked Questions

How can I make a reservation?

You can book via our website’s booking form, email us at reservations@ambururuwaterfalls.com, or call +254 721 202 551.

What is the best way to reach the resort?

The resort is accessible via the Kisumu-Siaya road. We also offer shuttle services from Kisumu International Airport. Contact us for details.

Do you offer event planning services?

Yes, we provide tailored event planning for weddings, conferences, and cultural festivals. Reach out to discuss your needs.

Are pets allowed at the resort?

Pets are not permitted to ensure the comfort of all guests and protect the local ecosystem.
